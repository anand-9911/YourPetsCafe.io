About Us

Hello, animal lovers and pet owners! You might have taken your pooches and kitties to pet friendly cafes, but there's now a new exclusive cafe for pets in town that is....human friendly. Yes, this cafe is especially for your furry babies to have fun, socialise and enjoy some mouthwatering treats, and there's food for you too. Located in Banjara Hills, The Pet Cafe is owned and run by Deven Baheti and Shruti Darak Mittal, who are actively involved in animal rescues across the city. The ambience is soft and chic, designed for the comfort of your pet. They have a bone shaped table, a counter where you can take polaroid pictures with your pet, a book shelf with books on animal care, and an entire room just for your puppers to play. We hear that they will also be hosting movie nights with pets, and let you book the space for pawesome parties. While your pets can enjoy their super meals, you can dig into the continental food here. They will also have vegan options, and we cannot wait to try it out!
